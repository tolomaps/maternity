This interactive map focuses on the conditions for women having children around the world. 

Metadata / Sources:
First item is name of column headers/attribute, followed by description, source, year, URL

1) Country: Country name

2) Code: Corresponding ISO country code

3) MaternalLeave: Workplace policies before and after childbirth - Length of Paid Maternal Leave
Source: World Policy Forum, Year Unknown, http://worldpolicyforum.org/tables/workplace-policies-childbirth/

4) PaternalLeave: Workplace policies before and after childbirth - Length of Paid Paternal Leave
Source: World Policy Forum, Year Unknown, http://worldpolicyforum.org/tables/workplace-policies-childbirth/

5) MaternalDeath: Lifetime risk of maternal death (1 in: rate varies by country)
Source: World Bank, 2013, http://datacatalog.worldbank.org/?Topics=Gender

6) FemaleLaborForceTotal: Labor force, female (% of total labor force)
Source: World Bank, 2012, http://datacatalog.worldbank.org/?Topics=Gender

7) FemaleLaborForceParticipationRate: Labor force participation rate, female (% of female population ages 15+) (modeled ILO estimate)
Source: World Bank, 2012, http://datacatalog.worldbank.org/?Topics=Gender

8) FertilityRate: Fertility rate, total (births per woman) 2012
Source: World Bank, 2012, http://datacatalog.worldbank.org/?Topics=Gender
